MEDUSA

Medusa is an organization through which you can sell or buy paintings from unknown beginners. If you are a painter and would like to sell your painting, contact us. You have our information in the contact section.

seller:

You will send personal information with a painting photo to our email address. After discussing with our analysts we will evaluate the price and quality of painting, we will send you a statement by meilom and we will see your work. You will receive our prize or set your own and put the painting on the web. After buying the painting we take 10% of the purchase price of the painting.

The buyer:

For each painting you have an email address and tel. Painting Owner's Number. You can negotiate with him. But if the painting is marked with M, it means that the painting belongs to Medus. In that case, you can't negotiate the price is solid, painting belongs to the organizations. Meduse M images will be displayed in our gallery at the contact address.
